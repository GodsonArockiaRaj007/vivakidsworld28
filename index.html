Empowering Your Child’s Journey from the First Step

At Viva Kids World, we offer four structured programs—Playgroup, Nursery, Jr.KG, and Sr.KG—each designed to meet the unique developmental needs of young learners. Our programs follow a child-centered approach, allowing children to grow at their own pace while building confidence and independence. From the moment a child enters our Playgroup, they are welcomed into a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment that encourages exploration and sensory experiences.

Through colors,textures, music, and movement, toddlers begin to develop their gross motor skills and social interactions, learning to express themselves through simple words, gestures, and creative play. As children progress to Nursery, they engage in more structured activities that strengthen pre-writing skills, language development, and cognitive abilities. With the introduction of puzzles, sorting games, and storytelling sessions, we help children develop curiosity and problem-solving skills while enhancing their communication.

Achievements

At Viva Kids World, we are committed to providing a holistic and enriching learning experience that nurtures every aspect of a child's development—academically, socially, emotionally, and creatively. We believe that early childhood education lays the foundation for a child's future, and our goal is to create an environment where young learners can explore, discover, and grow with confidence. Our approach goes beyond traditional academics; we integrate experiential learning, hands-on activities, and play-based methods to ensure that children develop essential life skills alongside their educational journey. Through engaging activities such as storytelling, music, art, movement, and role-playing, we stimulate their imagination and creativity while fostering critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
